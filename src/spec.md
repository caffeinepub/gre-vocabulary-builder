# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Populate the vocabulary database with 3000 GRE words to enable full functionality of the flashcard and practice test features.

**Planned changes:**
- Add 3000 GRE vocabulary words to the backend database
- Each word entry includes: word, definition, part of speech, example sentence, difficulty level, and category
- Ensure words are accessible through existing addWord and getWords functions

**User-visible outcome:** Users can browse, study, and practice with a complete library of 3000 GRE vocabulary words through flashcards and practice tests.
